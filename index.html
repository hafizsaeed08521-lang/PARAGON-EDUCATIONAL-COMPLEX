About Us

paragon educational complex offers a warm, modern day school and a safe boarding program for students who live farther away. Our boarding houses feel like a second home, with caring staff, cozy rooms, and lots of support for study and friendship.

We also offer an Islamic block dormitory where students can live in a calm, respectful environment that supports faith, learning, and brotherhood. The campus also includes a mosque for daily prayer, reflection, and community gatherings.

Our teachers are the heart of the school. They are inspiring, energetic, and always ready to help every student grow. They make learning fun, build confidence, and cheer on success in every classroom.

Programs

At paragon educational complex we run a wide range of programmes designed to develop the whole child. From faith-based learning and character education to technology, the arts, and sports, each programme is led by passionate teachers who bring lessons to life.

Our extra-curricular programmes include Qur'anic recitation circles, visual and performing arts workshops, competitive athletics teams, and many after-school clubs where students can explore robotics, music, drama, and community service. These programmes help students discover talents, build teamwork skills, and prepare for the future.

  • Qur'anic recitation
  • Visual and performing arts
  • Competitive athletics
  • After-school clubs and enrichment

Subjects We Teach

Our academic curriculum is broad and balanced. We give strong foundations in core academic subjects while encouraging creativity and digital skills.

  • Science
  • Mathematics
  • English
  • French
  • Creative Arts
  • Information & Communication Technology (ICT)
  • Qur'anic Studies

Each subject is taught with practical activities, hands-on projects, and regular assessments to make sure learners understand and can apply what they learn. Our teachers design engaging lessons that mix experiments, discussions, and creative tasks to keep learning exciting.

Campus & Blocks

Our campus is arranged so every student has space to learn, play, and grow. Below are the main areas you will find at paragon educational complex:

  • School Park — the green heart of the campus for outdoor learning and recreation
  • Islamic Block — junior high classrooms, labs, and teacher offices
  • Primary Block — bright, safe classrooms for younger learners
  • Library & Media Centre — books, study spaces, and computers
  • Science Labs — fully equipped for experiments and STEM work
  • Sports Field — for PE lessons, matches, and athletics practice

Visitors often comment on our friendly staff and well-kept grounds — everything is organised so students feel safe and inspired every day.
